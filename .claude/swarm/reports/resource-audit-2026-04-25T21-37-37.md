## Top concerns

1. **HIGH — Game3DPage chunk is 2.97 MB uncompressed** (1.01 MB gzipped). It's lazy-loaded, but cold-start of the 3D route still ships ~3 MB of JS. Single biggest bundling problem.
2. **HIGH — GamePage chunk is 1.95 MB uncompressed** (490 KB gzipped). Phaser-heavy. Lazy-loaded, but still nearly 4× the recommended 500 KB ceiling.
3. **MED — Vite emits unbundled audio assets totaling ~32 MB** in `build/game/`. Multiple alt mixes of `pixel_pedal_parade` (.mp2/v2/v3/v4) and large `.wav` originals are shipped alongside `.mp3` versions — looks like duplicate masters, not just format variants.
4. **MED — `three@0.170.0` is duplicated** because `stats-gl@2.4.2` (a `@react-three/drei` transitive) pins an old minor. Adds ~26 MB to `node_modules` and risks two `THREE` namespaces in the bundle.
5. **LOW — `npm dedupe --dry-run` would change 29 packages**, mostly minor patch bumps (`brace-expansion`, `jsonfile`, `ajv`, `body-parser`). Worth doing before release.

Total app footprint **1.20 GB** — under the 1.5 GB flag threshold, but the renderer alone is only 163 MB right now (likely before the user has opened the 3D scene).

### Findings

- **FAIL** Two chunks > 500 KB uncompressed: `Game3DPage` (2.97 MB) and `GamePage` (1.99 MB). Vite warned about this. Both are lazy-loaded via `React.lazy` in `App.jsx` — confirmed code-splitting *is* working (every lazy route in `App.jsx` lines 13–21 has a matching chunk in `build/assets/`). But the chunks themselves are too monolithic.
- **FLAG** `build/assets/` total 5.23 MB, just over the 5 MB threshold.
- `pixel_pedal_parade` exists in **5 variants** (no suffix, v2, v3, v4 .mp3, plus a quest_focus_hybrid.wav that may be related) totaling ~13 MB. Likely WIP audio drafts that should be culled before shipping.
- `garage_warm_oud.wav` (2.7 MB) and `neighborhood_hybrid_ride.wav` (2.7 MB) are uncompressed — re-encoding to 128 kbps OGG would cut each to ~250 KB.
- Lazy splitting confirmed: `App.jsx` contains 9 `React.lazy(...)` imports, all 9 produce distinct chunks in `build/assets/`.

### Build warning to address

`quizQuestionGenerator.js` is both statically imported (by `aiFallbacks.js`, `aiPromptBuilders.js`, `physicsEducationSystem.js`) **and** dynamically imported (by `MCPAIAdapter.js`). Vite says "dynamic import will not move module into another chunk" — meaning the dynamic import is wasted. Pick one strategy.

## Recommendations

**[HIGH] Split the Game3DPage and GamePage bundles** — Use `manualChunks` in `vite.config.js` to break out `phaser`, `three`, `@react-three/drei`, `@react-three/rapier`, and `@dimforge/rapier3d-compat` into their own vendor chunks. These will then be cached across reloads of those routes. Could move 1–1.5 MB of each route's bundle into shared chunks. *(effort: small)*

**[HIGH] Cull or re-encode the audio drafts** — `pixel_pedal_parade` has 4 versions totaling 11 MB; pick one. `.wav` files for `garage_warm_oud` and `neighborhood_hybrid_ride` should be `.mp3` or `.ogg`. Could shave 20+ MB off the build directory and the eventual installer. *(effort: small)*

**[MED] Resolve the `three` duplicate** — Add a `resolutions` (npm 8.3+: `overrides`) field to `package.json` forcing `three: 0.184.0` everywhere. Removes 26 MB of duplicate `stats-gl/node_modules/three`. *(effort: small)*

**[MED] Verify `@mediapipe/tasks-vision` and `hls.js` are actually used** — Both are 19 MB+ of optional functionality. If `@mediapipe` isn't wired into `src/renderer/game3d/`, drop it. If `hls.js` is just transitive from `react-youtube` and not invoked, it'll tree-shake out of the bundle but still bloats `node_modules` for devs. *(effort: small)*

**[MED] Fix the `quizQuestionGenerator.js` import inconsistency** — Either always-static or always-dynamic. Vite warns the dynamic import is wasted because of the static co-imports. *(effort: small)*

**[LOW] Run `npm dedupe`** — 29 packages would receive minor patch bumps. Worth doing before a release tag. *(effort: small)*

**[LOW] Add a `?perf=1` HUD to the 2D game** — A 30-line overlay showing `game.loop.actualFps`, `renderer.drawCount`, and pointer-tracked entity counts would let a future audit get runtime numbers without instrumentation. *(effort: small — separate task, NOT this agent)*

**[LOW] Watch the Vite handle count** — 25 353 OS handles on a single Vite process is unusually high. Likely benign (HMR + module graph), but if it climbs past ~50k mid-session, restart. *(effort: zero — observation only)*
