# First 15 Minutes Tutorial

## Scene 1: Game Start

Duration: 0-1 minute

Player action: Press any key to begin.

On screen: Title over a warm garage at dusk, soft dust in the light, Mr. Chen's workbench visible through the open door. Prompt: "Press any key."

Narration: "You're Zuzu. The neighborhood bridge is broken. Mr. Chen needs your help."

Special effects: Slow parallax on garage lights, evening cicadas, fade through the garage doorway.

## Scene 2: Wake Up in Garage

Duration: 1-3 minutes

Player action: Walk around the garage and discover that objects highlight when nearby.

On screen: Prompt: "Move with WASD or arrow keys." A small quest log tab appears but stays quiet.

Narration: "The garage smells like chain oil, warm rubber, and sawdust. Every tool looks like it has a story."

Special effects: Camera pans from wall tools to Zuzu, then settles into normal follow mode.

## Scene 3: Meet Mr. Chen

Duration: 3-5 minutes

Player action: Walk near Mr. Chen and press E when the interaction prompt appears.

On screen: Prompt: "Press E to talk." Dialogue panel opens with Mr. Chen portrait.

Dialogue: "Morning, Zuzu. Hear that squeak? This bike is telling us a little mystery. Want to help me solve it?"

Special effects: Mr. Chen wipes his hands, the bike wheel spins once with a squeak, quest banner appears: "Fix the Squeaky Chain."

## Scene 4: Find the Chain

Duration: 5-7 minutes

Player action: Follow the glowing hotspot, inspect the loose chain, and read the discovery.

On screen: Prompt: "Press E to inspect." Discovery card: "The chain is loose and rusty."

Dialogue: Mr. Chen says, "Good eye. A loose chain wastes your pedaling force before it reaches the wheel."

Special effects: Gentle golden glow around the chain, close-up pop-in of rusty links.

## Scene 5: Repair the Chain

Duration: 7-10 minutes

Player action: Click or confirm chain links in order to tighten them.

On screen: Mini-game panel with three highlighted links, tension meter, and "Tighten" feedback.

Dialogue: Mr. Chen says, "Small adjustments, then test. That is the rhythm."

Special effects: Chain links snap into alignment, bike wheel spins smoothly, reward popup: "+25 ZuzuBucks" and "Chain Hero."

## Scene 6: Next Steps

Duration: 10-15 minutes

Player action: Talk to Mr. Chen, accept the next hint, then exit the garage into the neighborhood.

On screen: Quest log tutorial opens briefly. Prompt: "Open Quest Log." Exit marker at garage door. Inventory tab flashes once when the chain oil is added.

Dialogue: "You fixed the chain. Now the neighborhood has a bigger problem: the bridge by the wash. Mrs. Ramirez saw the flood damage first. Go see what she noticed."

Special effects: Garage door light blooms, fade to neighborhood street with NPC silhouettes, music shifts from garage theme to neighborhood theme.

## Tutorial UI Prompts

- Movement tutorial: "Move with WASD or arrow keys."
- Interaction tutorial: "Press E to talk" and "Press E to inspect."
- Quest log tutorial: "New quest added. Open your quest log to see steps."
- Inventory tutorial: "Item added. Open inventory to view tools and supplies."
- Dialogue tutorial: "Press E or click to continue."
