# Zuzu Character Guide

## Core Identity

Zuzu is the protagonist of BikeBrowserWorld.

The parent-provided reference establishes the character as a joyful Sonoran dry-wash explorer: curious, muddy, brave, playful, and very much at home turning the outside world into an adventure.

The game sprite should be stylized and readable, not a photorealistic copy.

## Visual Anchors

- Short brown hair with a warm highlight.
- Bright playful yellow shirt with small red/blue pattern marks.
- Soft gray pants.
- Muddy shoes/ankles and occasional muddy hand detail.
- Explorer stick/staff silhouette for dry-wash and trail scenes.
- Upright, friendly, curious stance.

## Personality Read

At game scale, Zuzu should read as:

- curious first
- brave second
- kind always
- mechanically interested
- ready to get muddy

## Animation Notes

- Idle: tiny breathing bounce, alert head posture.
- Walk: light, energetic steps.
- Repair: hands-forward concentration pose.
- Discovery: slight lean-in before reward/quest feedback.

## Sprite Rules

- Preserve a consistent bottom-center anchor.
- Keep the yellow patterned shirt visible in every direction.
- Use mud as a story detail, not visual noise.
- Keep the stick optional by animation/state; it belongs especially in outdoor exploration.
- Avoid literal facial likeness. Capture the spirit and silhouette instead.
