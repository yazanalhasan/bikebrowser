# Relationship Memory Report

Generated: 2026-05-15

## Design Intent

The second humanization pass adds emotional memory without creating a reputation system. NPCs do not track affection scores or branch into large trees. They simply notice what Zuzu has already done.

The target feeling is:

> "I know these people, and they know what I have been working on."

## Added Memory Beats

### After Safety Check

Mrs. Ramirez:

- recognizes the safety-check habit
- keeps the tone practical and neighborly
- frames careful riding as normal, not heroic

Mr. Chen:

- notices Zuzu's careful eyes
- connects the safety habit to repair patience
- starts the chain repair through implication and listening

### After Chain Repair

Mr. Chen:

- hears the quieter chain
- names the repair process calmly
- lets the garage atmosphere carry the pride

Mrs. Ramirez:

- notices the improved chain sound
- references Mr. Chen as part of the neighborhood fabric
- adds a local curb and wind observation

### Returning To Completed Stations

The safety check and chain hotspot now respond quietly after completion:

- the safety bike still feels ready
- the chain turns clean

This prevents completed tasks from feeling emotionally reset.

## Ambient Neighborhood Dialogue

Ambient presence lines were added inside the existing NPC interaction scripts. They fire only on approach and use a cooldown to avoid chatter.

Tone rules:

- no quest commands
- no celebration speeches
- no tutorial language
- one short sensory or routine observation

## Local Identity Strengthening

New lines reference:

- cool evening rides
- breeze and curb awareness
- garage chain grease
- dust moving along the trail
- listening toward the river

These details keep the world specific to the warm Sonoran neighborhood and connected outdoor regions.

## Pacing Improvements

The new relationship lines are intentionally small:

- three lines for follow-up conversations
- one-line ambient remarks
- no large lore dumps
- no dramatic reveals

The emotional rhythm is quieter than a reward beat. NPCs mostly notice and continue being themselves.

## Remaining Robotic Risk

Some lower-priority NPCs still have first-contact dialogue only. They are warmer after pass 1, but they do not yet have relationship memory keyed to later quest completion.
