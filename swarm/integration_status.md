# Integration Status

## Current Phase

Convergence. The swarm should optimize for one authored experience, not maximum feature count.

## Integration Lane Stance

- Role: coordination, merge awareness, cohesion oversight.
- Not a feature lane: do not create new gameplay systems, regions, addons, or major art replacements.
- Protected systems remain stable unless a lane has explicit approval and a clear migration plan.

## Merge Preparation Checklist

- [ ] Every lane has updated `report_latest.md`.
- [ ] Every lane has listed touched files and scene/layout overlaps.
- [ ] Protected systems checked for unexpected changes.
- [ ] Duplicate visual, audio, UI, NPC, and storytelling beats identified.
- [ ] Validation evidence collected per lane.
- [ ] Integration pass chooses what to keep, soften, delay, or remove.

## Integration Decision Bias

When two good ideas compete, keep the one that improves first-read clarity and emotional tone with less surface area.
